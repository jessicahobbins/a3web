Our Work

Our teams background in remote sensing has provided us with the necessary skills to perform a variety of geometric processes on various types of imagery. Our team members understand the electromagnetic spectrum and can apply this to thematic mapping applications. We have the skills to apply our spectral target knowledge to extract thematic information from numerous types of imagery. The program we currently use for our work is PCI Geomatica. We have the skills and resources to register earth coordinates to imagery (aerial, satellite, etc.). Our team we can process and display your imagery using our image enhancement and image filtering techniques.
